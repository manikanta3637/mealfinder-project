Instructions:

Instructions:

Wash the eggplants thoroughly and slice them into thin, even rounds.

Soak the slices in salted water for about 15 minutes to reduce bitterness and remove excess moisture.

Pat the slices dry with a clean towel.

In a bowl, mix rice flour, corn flour, red chilli powder, turmeric, salt, and a pinch of garam masala.

Coat each slice in the dry mix, pressing gently to help it stick.

Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the slices on medium flame until both sides are crispy and golden brown.

Drain on a paper towel to remove excess oil.

Serve hot with chutney or as a side dish with rice and dal.
